SONR Music 2 — Firmware Flasher

Flash your device straight from the browser. No install needed. Use Chrome or Edge.

This browser doesn't support WebUSB. Open this page in Chrome or Edge on a computer (not a phone).

How to flash

  1. Hold the “+” button and plug in the USB cable.
  2. Check the device LED — it must be off. If it's lit, unplug and reconnect while holding “+”.
  3. Once the LED is off, release “+” and press “Update”.
  4. When the pop-up appears, press “+” once — that starts the update. Wait for the confetti 🎉.
